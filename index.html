Autobiography

Hi! I'm Frannieczeska Erene U. Tandang, a 20-year-old Bachelor of Science in Computer Science student at the University of the Philippines Los Baños. Growing up, I was always fascinated by how technology transforms the world, but it wasn't until I discovered the potential to integrate art into these innovations that my passion truly ignited. What started as curiosity about computers and gadgets evolved into a deep love for exploring their creative possibilities. When I learned that art could be seamlessly woven into emerging technologies—like through digital design, interactive media, or even AI-generated visuals—it sparked my interest in researching how to blend these fields. Now, as I study computer science, I'm diving into topics like computer graphics, user interface design, and creative coding, all while experimenting with tools such as Adobe Creative Suite and programming languages to bring artistic ideas to life.
